Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's a classic Italian dish that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan until browned.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the pan and sauté until onions are translucent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and water. Simmer the sauce for 20 minutes.
  4. Prepare the béchamel sauce in a separate pan.
  5. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish: meat sauce, pasta, béchamel, and cheese. Repeat.
  6. Bake in the oven until the top is golden and bubbly.
