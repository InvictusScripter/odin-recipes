Meat Pie

Description

This savory meat pie is a hearty and comforting dish, perfect for any occasion. Filled with a delicious combination of meat, vegetables, and spices, it's sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions, diced carrots, and cubed potatoes. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  3. Sprinkle flour over the meat and vegetable mixture. Stir well to combine.
  4. Pour in enough water or beef broth to create a thick gravy. Simmer until the mixture thickens.
  5. Stir in frozen peas and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Transfer the meat filling to a pie dish and cover with a layer of pie crust.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown.
  9. Allow the meat pie to cool slightly before serving.
